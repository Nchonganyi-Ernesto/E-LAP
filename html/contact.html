Frequently asked questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our products and services.

Do you offer free shipping?

Yes! We offer free standard shipping on all orders over $499. Express shipping options are available at an additional cost for those who need their components faster.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for all unopened products and a 14-day return policy for opened items (unless defective). All returns must be in original packaging with all accessories included.

Do you offer custom PC building services?

Absolutely! Our expert technicians can build a custom PC to your exact specifications. We offer free consultation to help you choose the right components for your needs and budget.

What warranty do you provide on your products?

Most products come with manufacturer warranties ranging from 1-3 years. Additionally, we provide a 1-year warranty on all custom builds and a 30-day warranty on refurbished products.

How long does it take to build a custom PC?

Standard custom builds take 3-5 business days for assembly and testing. Complex builds with custom liquid cooling or special modifications may take 7-10 business days.
